User Activity Monitor

Description

This project lets you know about your interaction with the page on real time. To do this, I used the new knowleadges about Rxjs, below you can find technical specifications about what things of Rxjs are specially used. Also you can test it using the demo.

Rxjs

Below you will find the operators I used and the reason, the utility.

  • fromEvent: to detect movements of the mouse and also the clicks. Click on fromEvent to get the documentation.
  • throttleTime: to reduce the frecuency of event emition, on this way I improved the performance of the monitor through do not filling the RAM. Click on throttleTime to get the documentation.
  • map: to get the data with specially type, ready to use for monitor. Click on map to get the documentation.

Demo

This demo simulates a real web page and in a card will show your clicks and position of mouse. Enjoy it!. 😎🔥😎🔥
